Sarah Hershenhouse

My name is Sarah Hershenhouse, and currently I'm a bartender. All of my jobs thus far have been customer service oriented, and I've been wanting to learn some kind of actually useful skill that would land me a good job. Upon doing some research, I discovered web development and decided I wanted to give it a try because it seemed more interesting than anything else I'd found. So far, I'm really enjoying the course. One of my goals in life is to be able to live a comfortable life and not have to worry about money while being self-sustainable. To me, the thought of not having to rely on anyone else seems very comforting. Of course, I'd still like to have people in my life that I can trust to be there for me. My hope is to finish this course and become a full time Web Developer.
